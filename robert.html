Robert Edward Harrison

Board Member
r.harisson@mapleleafharvest.org

Robert E. Harrison is a senior technical consultant, with 40 years’ experience in the field of automation systems. Having built control panels for a wide range of systems, from hydro power generation plants down to automated bakery production systems. He has developed, produced and installed computer system for corporate headquarters, sales offices to manufacturing plants and specialized departments. As a plant engineer he designed a complete manufacturing plant and all production equipment, managed the plant construction project, commissioned and then ran the plant. To complement his professional life, he has been a dedicated volunteer, with 46+ years of volunteer/community service, for local, provincial and national organizations. These included a wide range of volunteer activates from a Communications Officer on a Lake Ontario Marine rescue boat, serving on different Boards of Directors, producing and donating tens of thousands of dollars of signage to Not-For-Profit organizations to enhancing the charity’s public image. Robert’s greatest joy is involvement in organizations that make a difference in people’s lives and being a father to two great sons, Dylan and Corey.
